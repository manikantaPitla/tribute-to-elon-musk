ABOUT

Elon Musk is a renowned billionaire entrepreneur, investor, and engineer who has made significant contributions to various industries. He was born on June 28, 1971, in Pretoria, South Africa. Musk is widely recognized as the CEO and founder of SpaceX, Tesla, Neuralink, and The Boring Company, as well as a co-founder of PayPal.

ACHIEVEMENTS

1995 -

- ( Zip2 ) In 1995, Musk, his brother Kimbal, and Greg Kouri founded Zip2 an online business directory.

1999 -

- ( X.com and Paypal ) In 1999, Musk co-founded X.com and later renamed as Paypal, an online financial services and e-mail payment company.

2002 -

- ( SpaceX ) Musk founded SpaceX in May 2002 and became the company's CEO and Chief Engineer.

2004 -

- ( Tesla ) He invested $6.5 million, became the majority shareholder, and joined Tesla's board of directors as chairman.

2006 -

- ( SolarCity ) Founded by his cousins in 2006, Musk was their primary financial backer. Musk, via Tesla, acquired SolarCity in 2016 for $2.6 billion in stock and incorporated it into its operations as Tesla Energy.

2016 -

- ( Neuralink ) Musk co-founded Neuralink, a neurotechnology startup company, with an investment of $100 million. Neuralink aims to integrate the human brain with artificial intelligence

2017 -

- ( The Boring Company ) Musk founded the Boring Company to construct tunnels and revealed plans for specialized, underground, high-occupancy vehicles that could travel up to 150 miles per hour (240 km/h).

2022 -

- ( Twitter ) Musk started purchasing Twitter shares, reaching a 9.2% stake by April. He offered to purchase Twitter at $54.20 per share. The acquisition was officially completed on October 27.
